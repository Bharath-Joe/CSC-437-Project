Butter Chicken

Cuisine: Indian

Description: Butter Chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is a beloved North Indian dish featuring succulent chicken pieces marinated in a yogurt-spice blend, cooked to perfection and bathed in a velvety tomato-based gravy enriched with butter and cream. The harmonious fusion of aromatic spices like garam masala, cumin, and fenugreek leaves creates a rich and flavorful profile. The dish, known for its smoky undertones from traditional tandoor cooking, offers a perfect balance of savory and mildly sweet notes. Often enjoyed with naan or rice, Butter Chicken is a culinary masterpiece celebrated for its indulgent, comforting, and globally appealing taste.

Preperation Time: 45 mins to 1 hour

Utensiles

  • Cutting Board and Knife
  • Mixing Bowls
  • Cooking Pot or Pan
  • Tongs or Spatula
  • Measuring Spoons and Cups
  • Grater
  • Garlic Press
  • Pan for Browning Chicken
  • Spoon or Ladle
  • Small Frying Pan (optional)
  • Plates and Serving Dishes
  • Naan or Rice Server (optional)

Ingredients

  • Chicken (boneless, cut into cubes)
  • Yogurt
  • Tomatoes (pureed)
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Ginger (grated)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Kashmiri Red Chili Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Coriander Powder
  • Cumin Powder
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Kasuri Methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Oil
  • Fresh Coriander (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces with yogurt, Kashmiri red chili powder, turmeric powder, salt, and a splash of oil. Let it marinate for at least 1-2 hours or overnight for best results.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Saut√© until the onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add the pureed tomatoes to the pan and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  4. In a separate pan, heat butter. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it's browned on all sides.
  5. Transfer the browned chicken into the tomato-onion masala. Mix well and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes on low heat.
  6. Add garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, and kasuri methi to the chicken. Stir in cream to make the gravy rich and creamy. Adjust salt to taste.
  7. Continue to simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the gravy reaches your desired consistency.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve the Butter Chicken hot with naan or rice.
